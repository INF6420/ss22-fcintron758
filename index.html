Welcome to my website! Here you'll learn a little about me and my home. My name is Florence Frederick-Cintron. Currently, I am an elementary school teacher. I am also pursuing my MLIS Degree to become a librarian. I find joy in reading and learning new things. I enjoy traveling and trying new foods. The Virgin Islands consist of four islands. They are St. Croix, St. John, St. Thomas, and Water Island. St.Croix is the largest. Each island has its uniqueness that depends on what a vistor is looking for. All four islands are filled with crystal clear blue beaches and white sands. I live on the islands of St. Thomas. The capital of the Virgin Islands is Charlotte Amaile. St. Croix has two towns, Frederiksted and Christensted. St. John has one town, Cruz Bay. All four island have many unique and diverse activites to engage in. Visitors can enjoy hiking, golfing, swimming, fine dining, cultural activies and food on these islands. The islands are filled with a rich and diverse culture that is exemplified with the uniqueness of people you meet. The Virgin Islands is simply paradise!
